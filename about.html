About Us

OUR STORY

Verde Pizza Co. was born from a passion for creating exceptional plant-based pizzas that satisfy even the most devoted meat lovers. Founded in 2020 by Chef Maria Verde, our pizzeria combines traditional Italian techniques with innovative vegetarian ingredients to craft artisanal pies that are both sustainable and delicious. We source local, organic produce whenever possible and make our signature dough fresh daily using a 72-hour fermentation process that creates unmatched flavor and texture. Our cozy restaurant in the heart of downtown welcomes everyone—from veggie enthusiasts to curious carnivores—to experience pizza reimagined with creativity, conscience, and care. At Verde Pizza Co., we believe that great food can nurture both people and planet.
